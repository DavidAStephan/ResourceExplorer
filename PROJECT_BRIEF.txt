Fit on 2019-2023, validate on 2024-present. Report RMSE vs a naive
seasonal-random-walk benchmark.

Running nowcast at any date t within quarter Q:
- Observed PortWatch tonnage through day t
- Extrapolate remainder of the quarter using seasonal patterns and
  recent daily pace
- Apply bridge regression to get implied ABS monthly volumes
- Aggregate to quarterly
- Apply chain-volume conversion to produce real-term estimate

Uncertainty bands via bootstrap of bridge regression residuals, adjusted
for the share of the quarter already observed (shrinks as quarter
progresses).

3. **CSV exports** in `outputs/` for every run:
   - `nowcast_current.csv` — current quarter running estimate
   - `tonnage_daily.csv` — full daily series
   - `tonnage_monthly.csv` — aggregated monthly
   - `bridge_diagnostics.csv` — model fit stats
